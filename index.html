Our Services

Currently, Role Call is a web-based mobile application that connects tabletop RPG players with one another. No more sifting through thousands of forum threads or irrelevant social media posts to find your next campaign — Role Call puts the info you need to know in your hand.

Find Players

Make a player profile and start finding and private messaging likeminded tabletop RPG players near you on Role Call.

Group Up

Once you’ve found the members of your next campaign, create group chats with Role Call — keeping them either secret, private or public.

Share and Learn

Not sure where to start with tabletop RPGs? Connect with other Role Call users in our forums or start your own threads!
